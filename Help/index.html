FOLLOW THIS!

  • REDUCE

  • Minimise the amount of waste that you create.



  • REUSE

  • Reuse products, such as non-renewable plastic bags



  • RECYCLE

  • Recycle items like paper,plastic, electronics



  • The holy trinity of words that everyone says. If everyone follows REDUCE, REUSE, RECYCLE then we might have a better change in battling climate change.

  • Substitute your products with those that are environmental friendly.




  • Food Container

  • We all can use a glass tiffin box, or even use paper packaging.




  • Cutlery

  • In many places, especially in Indian marriages there is a VERY HIGH usage of plastic spoons. Bring your own spoon! Or use the metal once available at the plate table. TRY DOING THIS IN THIS SHAADI SEASON.




  • Batteries

  • Everyone uses batteries. 7.9 BILLION BATTERY USERS IN THE WORLD. Which amounts to TONS TONS of electronic waste. Dispose them carelessly, they start fires. Dispose them in the ocean, they do bad things. Try using recharable batteries, they are friendly on the pocket too.




  • Shopping bags

  • My local shop has started taking Rs.5 for plastic bags! They are getting costly day by day, BOTH ON POCKET AND ALSO ON THE NATURE. STOP BUYING PLASTIC BAGS. USE JUTE OR COTTON BAGS. THEY ARE RESUABLE AND LAST LONGER THAN PLASTIC ONES.
